Wandals - a professional tag typeface. 
Wandals is a high-quality graffiti typeface, drawn and created by Måns Grebäck during 2018.
It has multiple alternate alphabets, to be used to create a feeling of real handwriting. 
Activate contextual alternates and every letter will look different, with a final extended swash.

Wandals exists in three weights: Fat Wandals, Slim Wandals and Light Wandals

The family comes with a bonus Element font, containing decorative symbols to be used with the lettering.

The extensive set of characters covers all European Latin based languages.

Functions: Write for example --> or <--- for arrows. Write [01] to [39] for symbols. 
Writing multiple asteriskes *** or underlines _ _ _ also creates decorative elements.
